Plain Weave

The simplest and most common weaving technique. Threads cross over and under one another in a basic crisscross pattern.

Twill Weave

This technique creates a diagonal pattern and is used for strong, durable fabrics like denim.

Satin Weave

Produces a smooth, shiny surface. Used in luxury fabrics like satin and charmeuse.

Jacquard Weave

A complex weave that creates detailed patterns. Commonly used for brocade and damask fabrics.
